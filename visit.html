Policies

COAT & BAG CHECK

Coat check is open. Please see check all coats and large bags.

CELL PHONES

Please set your phone to silent mode.

STROLLERS

Strollers are permitted in the Museum, but stroller access may be limited in certain exhibitions pending space limitations. Please call ahead to confirm.

SKETCHING

Use of writing utensils other than pencils is prohibited in the exhibition rooms.

CAMERAS

Photography is permitted in the Museum galleries unless otherwise noted. Videotaping and tripods are prohibited.

FOOD & DRINK

Use of closed water bottles is permitted in areas outside of the Museum’s galleries.

Accessibility

Please inquire at the Visitor Desk for any assistance you may require.

HEARING

Assistive listening devices, headsets, and neck loops are available for most programs at the yeesiàsià gallery.

WHEELCHAIRS

The gallery is wheelchair accessible. Two wheelchairs are available in the Coat Check free of charge. Please call in advance to reserve one if you need a wheelchair during your visit.

Route

BUS

Take bus number X from A to the museum. The bus stop is located at street and intersection, near landmark. You can check the bus schedule or use a transportation app to plan your trip. Once you get off the bus, the gallery is just a short walk away. You can follow the signs or ask a local for directions if you need help finding it.

SUBWAY

Take the MRT from location to station. You can check the MRT map or use a transportation app to plan your route. When you get off at the station, follow the signs or ask a station employee for directions to the gallery. The gallery is just a short walk from the station.

CAR

From major road, take exit num and follow the signs to the gallery. The gallery is located at address and has ample parking available in the surrounding area. You can use a map or navigation app to plan your route and find the best place to park. Please be aware of any parking restrictions or fees that may apply.

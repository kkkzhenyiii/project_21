VISITOR POLICIES

COAT & BAG CHECK

Coat check is open. Please see check all coats and large bags.

CELL PHONES

Please set your phone to silent mode.

STROLLERS

Strollers are permitted in the Museum, but stroller access may be limited in certain exhibitions pending space limitations. Please call ahead to confirm.

SKETCHING

Use of writing utensils other than pencils is prohibited in the exhibition rooms.

CAMERAS

Photography is permitted in the Museum galleries unless otherwise noted. Videotaping and tripods are prohibited.

FOOD & DRINK

Use of closed water bottles is permitted in areas outside of the Museum’s galleries.

Accessibility

Please inquire at the Visitor Desk for any assistance you may require.

HEARING

Assistive listening devices, headsets, and neck loops are available for most programs at the yeesiàsià gallery.

WHEELCHAIRS

The gallery is wheelchair accessible. Two wheelchairs are available in the Coat Check free of charge. Please call in advance to reserve one if you need it during your visit.

ROUTE

Bus

Take bus number X to the museum. The bus stop is located at street X and Y intersection, near the Z landmark. Once getting off the bus, please follow the signs to the gallery.

Subway

Take the MRT X-line to Y station. When getting off at the station, please follow the signs to the gallery.

Car

From major road, take exit num and follow the signs to the gallery. There are ample parking lots in the surrounding area.

POUR PAINTING

05/01 - 08/03

Pour painting is a unique form of art where artists use different colors and materials to pour paint onto a canvas, allowing it to freely flow and form rich and colorful visuals through the forces of gravity and motion. This exhibition invites multipl e pour painting artists to showcase their unique ideas and techniques in creation, providing visitors with an immersive experience to appreciate the charm of art through the fluidity and transformation of colors.

Rendering is a technique of using colors to represent changes in space and light. Through clever composition, painting techniques, and rich imagination, painters bring static objects to life and present colorful scenes that bring visual and spiritual pleasure to viewers. In this exhibition, we hope to explore the charm of colors in flux through the works of multiple artists, leading visitors into a world full of colors and emotions.

ARTISTS: Ryan Church, Jama Jurabaev, Vitaly Bulgarov, Anthony Jones, Raphael Lacoste, Ian McQue, Maciej Kuciara, Sparth (Nicolas Bouvier)
